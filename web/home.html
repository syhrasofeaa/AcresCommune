Our History

Founded in 2010, Acres Commune started as a small group of passionate farmers dedicated to sustainable agriculture. Over the years, it has grown into a thriving community that embraces innovative farming techniques and collaborative efforts. Our mission is to support local agriculture and create a sustainable future for the next generations.
